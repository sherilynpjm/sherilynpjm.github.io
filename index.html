I am Ash

FILM BOOKLET

Explanation of project:
For this project, we are required to graphically reinterpret a classic film.

Chosen film:
‘Fantastic Mr Fox’ by Wes Anderson

Concept:
There were many parallels that we could be drawn between the movie ‘Fantastic Mr Fox’ and social issues that exist in our today’s reality. From stealing to greed to anger, selfishness and bullying. Among all the important issues that could be addressed, there was one issue that stood out to me the most. It was the life of Mr Fox’s son, Ash.

In this film, Ash is portrayed as different, just because he does not meet up to the standard of an ideal child as compared to his cousin kristofferson. Hence the focus of my filmbooklet would be the comparison between ash and his cousin Kristofferson.
